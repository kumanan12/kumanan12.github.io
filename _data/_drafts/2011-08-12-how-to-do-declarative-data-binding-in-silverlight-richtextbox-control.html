In my project, I had a scenario where I need to use RichTextbox(RTB) in a gridview and bind it declaratively to a Paragraph. After spending lot of hours on the internet looking for solutions, I found one link which was close to what I wanted and I must refer that link here.

http://forums.silverlight.net/p/186209/425832.aspx

The data that will be passed to the richtextbox is of type IEnumerable>. This data need to be converted and styled into a paragraph and passed to the RTB. The Solution to this problem can solved in three steps.

1. Create a ValueConverter that converts IEnumerable> into a Paragraph.

2. Create a Silverlight user control and put the RTB inside it.

3. Create a Dependency Property . I called in ParagraphContent and bind it to the RTB. This step may look confusing. Once you see the code, you will get the idea.
